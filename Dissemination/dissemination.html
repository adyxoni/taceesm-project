ELEMEND project presented in 'Elektrodistribucija' a.d Pale

On Monday, 28.12.2020. the team from the University of East Sarajevo had a chance to demonstrate the new master program to the staff of the branch office of the ‘Elektrodistribucija’ Pale.
ELEMEND at Inter-project coaching online meeting – TRAFSAF

On 30.09.2020., an Inter‐project coaching online meeting within the ERASMUS+ project TRAFSAF was organized with the purpose of exchange of experiences in different ERASMUS + projects.

TCASU was invited to attend the meeting and to represent the ELEMEND project. On this occasion, the Project has been described in detail, and the information regarding the project goals, outcomes, and impact was provided.
